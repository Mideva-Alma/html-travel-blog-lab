Adventures in Fire Island

My trip to Fire Island was full of beautiful beaches, peaceful trails, and stunning sunsets. The highlights included long walks along the shoreline, exploring the historic lighthouse, and enjoying the calm ocean breeze. It was an unforgettable adventure! I would highly recommend visiting this serene destination for anyone looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.
